Refunds 🔗

If you didn’t like our platform, you have 30 calendar days to request a complete refund on your subscription from the date you activated it. To request a refund, please contact us with the email address you used to sign up.

We limit one refund per customer per product. If you’ve already been refunded for a membership in the past, you are not eligible for another refund.
